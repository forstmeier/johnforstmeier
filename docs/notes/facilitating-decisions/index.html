Note - Facilitating Decisions

> 2020-04-23

The individuals best suited for making decisions on any solution are those closest to the problem. It is the leader’s only job to facilitate these decisions.

  • Provide guidance when it is requested
  • Arbitrate disputes when they arise
  • Make decisions when presented options
  • Spread vision and purpose when leading

Leaders should work to empower individuals to make their own decisions.

To lead is to serve.

About

John is a senior full stack engineer at Shipyard and founder at Automated Capital. His personal activities include working on side projects, writing, reading, and lifting.

John is interested in early-stage startups working on platforms in the nuclear, farming, manufacturing, and space sectors. He is pro-West, pro-growth, and pro-human.

John can be reached via direct message on Twitter. He enjoys collaborating with similarly-interested individuals.
